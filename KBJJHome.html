Discover the Gentle Art of Jiu-Jitsu

Our academy offers specialized programs for adults, women and children in one of the most effective martial art and self defense systems in the world. We are located in Kapolei, Hawaii and welcome new and current students to train at our facility.

Fundamental Jiu-Jitsu Program

Our goal is to tailor beginners with our Fundamental Jiu-Jitsu Program. We focus on the essential Jiu-Jitsu techniques and concepts that will build a solid self-defense and Jiu-Jitsu foundation. Our program is focused on the Pedro Sauer Jiu-Jitsu White to Blue Belt Curriculum.

How it Started?
Shane Agena received his black belt from Relson Gracie in 2009. He began his journey with Relson Gracie at the Main Academy, training, competing and teaching Jiu-Jitsu since 1996. Shane continually demonstrates his passion for Jiu-Jitsu and strives to pass on his knowledge to his students. His goal is to continue producing top level competitors and help change people’s lives for the better.

De La Riva
